Tlala
Tlala is a interactive complex built to be a special destination for both individual and families.
1. What is Tlala ?
Tlala is a modern urban commercial project. It brings together an elite of Saudi and international brands under one roof.
2. About Tlala
With the aim of building a coherent mix that improves the visitor experience within an integrated environment in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia..
3. Tlala Complex
The complex consists of three main areas
1 - shopping area
2 - entertainment areas (to host concerts, and cultural activities)
3 - area designated for entrepreneurs (co-working spaces).
4. Tlala Additional services
Additional services:
Automated teller machine
Parking lot
- 24/7 protection.
